Terminal Advantages

Strategic Location

Just 6 km from the Sea Trading Port of Chornomorsk, our terminal is adjacent to the port station, enabling fast and efficient export routes.

Railway Infrastructure

We operate our own railway line with a daily intake capacity of 120 railcars, ensuring seamless grain delivery and distribution.

High Loading Throughput

Equipped with modern transshipment technologies, the terminal handles vessels up to 82,000 DWT with a loading rate of 10,000–13,000 tons per day.

Our Services

Grain Intake

Automated and fast grain acceptance from railcars and trucks with quality checks and dust-free handling.

Safe Storage

Modern silos with advanced monitoring for temperature and moisture. Capacity: 115,000 tons of cereals and oilseeds.

Our Partners

We cooperate with leading agricultural companies and exporters in Ukraine and abroad.
